FSTTCS'23 (Dec 15-20) at IIITH

FSTTCS 2023 is the 43rd conference on Foundations of Software Technology and Theoretical Computer Science. It is organised by IARCS, the Indian Association for Research in Computing Science, in association with ACM India. It is a forum for presenting original results in foundational aspects of Computer Science and Software Technology.

FSTTCS 2023 will be held in IIIT Hyderabad during December 18–20, 2023. The conference is being organized as an in-person event.
